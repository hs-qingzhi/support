Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

Ashore is a local-first habit companion. We keep data collection to the bare minimum as a design principle.

1. What we store

Your goals, check-in records, savings settings and reminder preferences are kept only on your own device. They are never uploaded to our servers. (Exception: when you choose to use Feedback, the content you submit is sent to our feedback server — see Section 7; we also integrate third-party analytics and push SDKs that, after you consent, collect limited anonymous information — see Section 8.)

2. What we don't collect

We don't collect your name, phone number, email, contacts, photos, location, or any information that could identify you, and we include no third-party advertising SDKs. The Umeng analytics and push SDKs we integrate collect only anonymous usage and device information and are never used for cross-app advertising tracking — see Section 8. (Content, images and optional contact information you voluntarily submit through Feedback are an exception — see Section 7.)

3. About subscriptions

When you buy a subscription, payment is handled by Apple; we never see your payment details. To confirm whether your Pro access is active, your device sends Apple's signed purchase record to our own server, which validates it with Apple and stores only the purchase identifiers Apple returns — never your name or payment details, and not tied to your real identity. See Apple's privacy policy for details.

4. Notifications

The daily reminder is a local notification on your device, turned on by you, and its content passes through no server.

5. Your data is yours

You can export all of your data anytime under Settings - Data, and you can delete a goal or uninstall the app to clear your local data. Export and backup are free forever and are never locked behind a subscription.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions about privacy, you can reach us through the in-app feedback under Settings - About - Feedback & contact.

7. Feedback

When you use the feedback under Settings - About - Feedback & contact, we collect and process the following solely to receive and handle your feedback, communicate with you, and improve our products: the feedback content, images and contact information (optional, stored encrypted) you provide; and, to help diagnose issues, your app version, OS version, device model, language, membership status, current page and an anonymous device identifier (which does not contain your real identity such as name or phone number); your access IP is also recorded at submission solely for abuse prevention and is not retained long-term. Data is stored on Alibaba Cloud (mainland China, Beijing); images are uploaded to Alibaba Cloud Object Storage (OSS) and machine-moderated by the Alibaba Cloud DashScope vision model to block non-compliant content. Images are kept for 12 months by default and text feedback for 12 months after the ticket is resolved, then cleared automatically; you can delete all of your feedback anytime on the feedback page. This channel is a private support ticket between you and the developer; its content is never shown publicly to other users.

8. Third-party SDKs (analytics and push)

To understand how features are used, measure retention and keep improving the app, and (on iOS only) send you reminder notifications, we integrate the Umeng+ SDK (provided by Umeng Tongxin (Beijing) Information Technology Co., Ltd., part of Alibaba Group) after you accept this policy: Umeng analytics (U-App, iOS and Android) collects anonymous usage and event data, app and OS version, device model, network type, and an anonymous device identifier, for statistics and retention analysis; Umeng push (U-Push, iOS only) collects the device push token needed to deliver notifications, used to send you reminders. None of this includes information that identifies you, such as your name or phone number, and it is never used for cross-app advertising tracking; the SDK is initialised only after you consent. You can turn off notifications anytime in system settings. Umeng's privacy policy: https://www.umeng.com/page/policy.
